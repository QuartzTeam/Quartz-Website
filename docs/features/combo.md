# Combo

A combo counter overlay that tracks your current hit streak, with color, size, and shadow settings independent of the game's own combo display.

## Where to find it

Open the Quartz menu in-game and pick **Overlay → Combo** from the sidebar.

!!! note
    The **Enable Overlays** master switch at the top of the Overlay tab controls every overlay HUD — Panels, Progress Bar, Combo, Judgement, Song Title, and Key Viewer. Combo won't show if that's off.

## Highlights

- **Caption** — an optional label shown alongside the count, with its own offset, size, and shadow.
- **Color** — a low-combo and high-combo color that blend up to **Color Max Combo**, plus an optional **Perfect Color (at Max)** override once you hit the ceiling, or a flat **Solid Color** instead of the gradient.
- **Pulse** — the counter pops on every hit; **No Pop Animation** turns that off, and **Pulse Duration** / **Count Pulse Scale** / **Label Pulse Offset Y** tune the animation itself.
- **XPerfect Combo (X Only)** — when the XPerfect mod is installed alongside Quartz, counts only X-Perfect hits toward the combo instead of every hit.
- **Combo Counts Auto Hits** — include autoplay-driven hits in the combo count.

!!! tip
    Drag Combo (and every other overlay) into place with the **Reorganize** button at the top of the Overlay tab.
