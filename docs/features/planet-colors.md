# Planet Colors

Recolors the planets and their tails, overriding whatever a level's own events or skin would otherwise set.

## Where to find it

Open the Quartz menu in-game, go to the **Visuals** tab, and open the **Planet Colors** section.

## Highlights

- Independent **color** and **ball opacity** for each of the four planets.
- **Separate Tail Color** — off makes tails use the ball color (with their own opacity); on gives each planet's tail its own color.
- **Recolor Ring** — paints the planet ring a custom color; when off, the ring is hidden entirely while Planet Colors is active.
